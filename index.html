Customer data in financial institutions is fragmented across core banking systems, loan origination platforms, CRM tools, service desks, and digital channels. Financial institutions handle massive volumes of customer inquiries across email, voice, and chat channels. Delays and inaccuracies arise due to staffing constraints, complex regulations, training gaps, and reliance on legacy systems.
